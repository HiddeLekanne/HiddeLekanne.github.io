◆ walkMode

rWalkGenerator::WalkMode rMotionRequest::walkMode

walkMode sets the type of walk to be used.

This can be either one of: rWalkGenerator::walkMode::speedMode - Speed in mm/s and radians/s. rWalkGenerator::walkMode::stepSizeMode - Step size in mm and radians (robot will do exact steps). rWalkGenerator::walkMode::targetMode - UNDER DEVELOPMENT. Walk to given relative position in mm and radians.
