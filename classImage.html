Detailed Description

Image: class which loads in a raw image buffer from the camera device and constructs several formats and sizes form this image.

Formats are YUV, HSV and RGB and sizes are Full and downsampled with DOWN_SAMPLE_RATE. Combinations are YUV both, BGR downsampled and HSV downsampled. Also the compression and decompression of the YUV channel is implemented here.
