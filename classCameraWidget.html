Detailed Description

CameraWidget: Widget that shows the images from the upper of lower camera, ball, lines and field boundary.

Also supports calibration of top camera rotation: https://github.com/IntelligentRoboticsLab/DNT2017/wiki/Camera-Calibration

Author
: Sbastien
: Caitlin
: Hidde
