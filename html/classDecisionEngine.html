Detailed Description

Lazily selects a Decision with the highest score from an activated subset.

The DecisionEngine selects the optimal Decision, based on its Decision::computeScore. Each Decision is associated with an Event. By raising and clearing an Event, you load and unload the associated Decisions into the set of active Decisions.
