Detailed Description

rRobotStatusSensor: representation that holds the status of the battery and the CPU. Battery current is in Ampere. Battery charge is in percentage (0 to 1). Battery temperature is in percentage (0 to 1) unknown relative to what.

CPU temperature is in Celsius.
