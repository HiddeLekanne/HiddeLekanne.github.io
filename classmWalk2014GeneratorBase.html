Detailed Description

This file declares the UNSW 2014 walk generator.

It was refactored to fit into the B-Human framework. This version is the DNT implementation of the BHuman/UNSW walk 2014 generator.

Original authors: Bernhard Hengst, Thomas Rfer

Author
Thomas Groot

The method determines the forward, left, and lift offsets of both feet.

The method distiguishes between the swing foot and the support foot.

Parameters
swingFootSignA sign based on the swingFoot (1 : left is swing foot, -1 right is swing foot).
forwardSwing0Forward offset of the current swing foot when the support changed (in m).
forwardSupport0Forward offset of the current support foot when the support changed (in m).
forwardSwingThe new forward offset of the swing foot is returned here (in m).
forwardSupportThe new forward offset of the support foot is returned here (in m).
leftSwingThe new sideways angle of the swing foot is returned here (in radians).
leftSupportThe new sideways angle of the support foot is returned here (in radians).
footHeightSwingThe new lift offset of the swing foot is returned here (in m).
footHeightSupportThe new lift offset of the support foot is returned here (in m).
