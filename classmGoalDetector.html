Detailed Description

rGoalDetector: This module finds goalpost by looking at the hsv image. It uses the boundary found in field-filter to identify potential pole bases. From those bases the vertical pole is found by checking for a continuous sequence of pixel values in range (-MAX_WHITE_VARIATION_ to MAX_WHITE_VARIATION) of the base. Potential poles are clustered together, only the largest pole per cluster is used to check for shape and a top bar. Shape is the dimensional proportions the real object has to accomodate according to the manual. Top bar is a continues line of white pixels from the top of the larger pole to the smaller pole.
