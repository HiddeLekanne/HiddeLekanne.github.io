Detailed Description

Detect the current physical position of the robot using the gyro (for falling), foot fsr sensors (for lifting) and joint angles (for sitting).

Possible states: upright, falling, fallen, sitting or lifted

This module will also play a sound when the robot is lifed in the air. If the sound plays too often during a certain stance, you can add "play_sound = false;" to that case.
